Introducing Sparc

SPARC is the technical fest by the students of VESIT MCA and is a platform for the upcoming talents of all the MCA colleges in Mumbai. Started in the year 2009, SPARC organizes a bouquet of both technical and non-technical events. SPARC provides a social platform to the students from various colleges to come together and share their knowledge and experiences.

About Us

VESIT (Vivekananda Education Society’s Institute of Technology) has been founded upon the principles of Swami Vivekananda. VESIT has been teaching MCA since last 7 years.

Stimulating Performance And Redefining Creativity (SPARC) is VESIT’s technical festival organized by the MCA department. It was first organized in 2008-09 by a group of enthusiastic individuals who made this festival a success and set an example for the juniors to follow. SPARC includes a range of technical and non-technical events in which students from various MCA colleges participate and compete to share and gain knowledge and experience.
This is the official website of the festival where you will find all the details one may want to know. For further assistance, you may contact us.
